$\large{\log_3{(x + 3 - \frac{9}{x})} \leq \log_3{\bigg(x^2 + 3 * x + \frac{1}{x} - 10\bigg)}}$

$x^3 + 2 * x^2 - 13 * x + 10 \geq 0$

$x^3 - x^2 + 3 * x^2 - 3 * x - 10 * x + 10 \geq 0$

$(x - 1) * (x^2 + 3 * x - 10) \geq 0$

$(x - 1) * (x - 2) * (x + 5) \geq 0$
