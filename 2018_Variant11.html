, 11 16.

A bus runs daily from the district center to the village. The probability that on Monday there will be less than 17 passengers on the bus is 0.85.

The probability that there will be less than 11 passengers is 0.52.

Find the probability that the number of passengers will be from 11 to 16.

$A = [0,11) \cup B = [11,16] \equiv C = [0;16]$

$p_A + p_B = p_C \implies p_B = 0.85 - 0.52 = 0.33$
