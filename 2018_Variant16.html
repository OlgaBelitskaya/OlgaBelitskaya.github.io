44/2 = 22.

11 / 22 = 0.5. $\implies$ $2 * 30^o = 60^o$.

. $\implies$ $180^o - 60^o = 120^o$.

The half of the rhombus's dagonal is 44/2 = 22.

The sine of the half of the acute angle in the rhombus is 11/22 = 0.5. $\implies$ The acute angle of the rhombus is $2 * 30^o = 60^o$.

Any rhombus is a parallelogram. $\implies$ The obtuse angle of the rhombus is $ 180 ^ o - 60 ^ o = 120 ^ o $.
